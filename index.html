WORLD OF MOVIES

OUR TOPIC :

The project name is The World of Movies. The aim of our project is to enable people to easily learn about the movies they want to watch.

OUR PLAN :

At the planning stage, the project idea was first determined. It was decided by the team which steps should be followed in order to implement the idea. First, the appearance of the website was designed as a draft, then what was required for the front-end part was discussed and a decision was made. Finally, the data access part was discussed in order to combine the website and the database, and the plan of the project process was roughly agreed within the team.

OUR TASK DISTRIBUTION :

The task distribution was made within the team in order to deal with the Doğukan - İsmail database part, the Berfu - Helin web part and finally the Serkan data accessories part within the team.
